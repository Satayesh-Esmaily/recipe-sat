Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic, onions, and herbs for seasoning

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in boiling water until tender.
  3. Prepare the ground beef with tomato sauce and seasonings.
  4. Layer the noodles, ricotta cheese, and meat sauce in a baking dish.
  5. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes, then sprinkle with parmesan and bake for 5 more minutes.

Qabili Pilau

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g lamb, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup almonds
  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter
  • Spices: cinnamon, cumin, cardamom, cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cook the lamb until browned and tender.
  2. Fry the onions in ghee until golden, then add the spices.
  3. Add the rice to the pot and cook for a few minutes before adding water to cook the rice.
  4. Once rice is cooked, layer it with the lamb, carrots, raisins, and almonds.
  5. Let it simmer for another 15 minutes before serving.

Mantu

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 pack of dumpling wrappers
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Fry onions in oil until golden, then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  2. Mix in tomato paste, cumin powder, salt, and pepper, then set aside to cool.
  3. Fill dumpling wrappers with the beef mixture, fold them, and steam for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Serve with a tomato sauce or yogurt topping.

Ashak

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of dumpling wrappers
  • 2 cups leeks, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 200g ground lamb or beef
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • Yogurt for topping

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Fry leeks in oil until soft, then add ground meat and cook until browned.
  2. Add garlic, mint, salt, and pepper to the mixture and set aside to cool.
  3. Fill the dumpling wrappers with the mixture and steam for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Serve with tomato sauce and a spoonful of yogurt.

Bolani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Oil for frying

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, water, and salt to make a dough, then let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. For the filling, mix mashed potatoes with green onions, cilantro, cumin, and salt.
  3. Roll out the dough and fill with the potato mixture, then fold the dough to seal it.
  4. Fry the filled dough in hot oil until golden brown on both sides.
